Research Portfolio

Experimental studies focused on sustainable crop systems and locally available resources in Rwanda's agricultural context.

Thesis — May 2026

Effect of Different Substrates (Wheat Straw, Saw Dust & Maize Cobs) on Growth and Yield of Oyster Mushrooms

Case study: Musanze. Investigated the performance of Pleurotus ostreatus on three locally sourced organic substrates at the University of Rwanda, Busogo Campus. Evaluated yield parameters, biological efficiency, and substrate suitability under Busogo climatic conditions.

📐 CRBD · ANOVA · LSD · Supervisor: Ir. Alphonsine D-KENYANGI (Msc)
Research Study

Aeroponic, Sandponic & Conventional Screen House Systems for Seed Potato Production

Comparative study evaluating aeroponic and sandponic technologies against conventional screen house methods for mini-tuber seed potato production. Assessed stolon count, tuber yield, plant vigor, and production efficiency under controlled conditions at Busogo Campus.
